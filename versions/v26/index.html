Decision doctrine

Evidence before momentum

Assurance answers what must be owned, evidenced, permitted, corrected, and verified for an advancing project to remain acceptable.

Independent status axes

Condition, evidence, and workflow states remain separate. A missing report is not a confirmed failure, and a received report is not verified evidence.

Non-compensatory gates

Failed hard gates, critical evidence defects, critical incidents, and noncompliance control the decision before comparative suitability or optimization scores.

Explicit consequence

Every material record identifies its owner, reviewer, due milestone, acceptance criteria, affected outputs, and blocking consequence.

Prototype boundary: This static environmental-assurance workflow organizes requirements, evidence metadata, owners, decisions, and consequences. It does not provide secure document management, a tamper-proof audit trail, legal compliance, professional approval, agency acceptance, community consent, or multiuser enterprise recordkeeping.

Data and Recovery

Static, local, and transparent

This shell stores no project data. Structured local persistence, migration, import/export, reset, backup, and quarantine behavior arrive in V29.

Self-contained delivery

No build system, server, authentication service, or external database is required. Relative links remain compatible with GitHub Pages and local use.

Evidence boundary

Future evidence controls store metadata and references only. The public HTML does not claim secure custody of professional reports.
